### AC-1 â€” Capture Spoolman `filament.extra` (+ `filament.extra.url`) on the internal model â€” the data the mapping reads

- `filament.extra.url` (the purchase link, inherited per Decision AH) is **carried** as one key inside `extra` â€” no separate field, no display logic (that is Story 32.6). 32.5 does NOT parse/validate the URL; it is along for the ride.
- This is an **append-only model change** (one new field with a safe default): every existing Spoolman read keeps working; the public DTOs in `schemas.py` are **NOT** extended (the `extra` dict is internal-only â€” it does NOT flow to the `/api/spools/*` response, which is `extra="forbid"`; AC-9 keeps `schemas.py` byte-identical).
